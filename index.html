Crypto Castle Incubator is an open-source app that connects users who want to earn rewards for working together to improve the JMES cryptocurrency.

Any kind of work that aligns with our strategy can be incentivized, and worked on together with full transparency.

Whether you want to propose ideas, develop code, manage & promote projects, or improve the Incubator app itself, check the bounties below or join the Crypto Castle Devs Discord server to chat with like-minded people in the #incubator channel.
